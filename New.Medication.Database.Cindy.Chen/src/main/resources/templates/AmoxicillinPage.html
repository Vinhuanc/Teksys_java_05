Medications

Amoxicillin

Generic Name: Amoxicillin

Brand Name: Amoxil

Medication Class: Penicillin

Main Purpose: Antibiotic

Prescription Quantity: 20368921

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1972

Introduction: Amoxicillin is an antibiotic used to treat a number of bacterial infections. These include middle ear infection, strep throat, pneumonia, skin infections, and urinary tract infections among others.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Stomach changes, Chest pain, Bloating, Headache

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Constipation, Leg pain
