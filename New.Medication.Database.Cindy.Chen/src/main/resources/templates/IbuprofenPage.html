Ibuprofen

Generic Name: Ibuprofen

Brand Name: Advil

Medication Class: NSAID

Main Purpose: Pain

Prescription Quantity: 10951995

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1961

Introduction: Ibuprofen is a medication in the nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) class that is used for treating pain, fever, and inflammation.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Thirst, Headache, Vision change, Sleep change

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Fluid retention, Ankle swelling
