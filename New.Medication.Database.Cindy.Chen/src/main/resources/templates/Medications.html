Atorvastatin


Lipitor


Atorvastatin is in a class of medications called HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors (statins). It works by slowing the production of cholesterol of the heart, brain, and other parts of the body.

Amoxicillin


Amoxil


Amoxicillin is an antibiotic used to treat a number of bacterial infections. These include middle ear infection, strep throat, pneumonia, skin infections, and urinary tract infections among others.

Lisinopril


Prinivil


Lisinopril is an angiotensin-converting enzyme(ACE) inhibitor used in the therapy of hypertension and heart failure. Lisinopril works by decreasing chemicals that tightens the blood vessels.

Levothyroxine


Synthroid


Levothyroxine is used to treat hypothyroidism. It is also used with surgery and radioactive iodine therapy to treat thyroid cancer. Levothyroxine is in a class of medications called hormones.

Albuterol


Ventolin


Albuterol is used to prevent and treat difficulty breathing, wheezing, shortness of breath, coughing, and chest tightness caused by lung diseases, such as asthma, and obstructive pulmonary disease.

Metformin


Glucophage


Metformin is a biguanide oral hypoglycemic used primarily for treating type 2 diabetes mellitus. Metformin is associated with improved all-cause and cardiovascular mortality1.

Amlodipine


Norvasc


Amlodipine is commonly used in the treatment of high blood pressure and angina. It lowers blood pressure by relaxing the blood vessels so the heart does not have to pump as hard, reduces Nitric Acid.

Metoprolol


Lopressor


Metoprolol is an treatment for angina, heart failure, myocardial infarction, atrial fibrillation/flutter, and hypertension. Metoprolol is also used to lower risk of death or heart failure.

Omeprazole


Prilosec


Omeprazole is a proton pump inhibitor used to treat symptoms of GERD, such as frequent heartburn and gastric acid hypersecretion. It can decrease the amount of acid produced, and promote healing of tissues.

Losartan


Cozaar


Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) used to treat hypertension.It can reduce the risk of stroke in patients with high blood pressure with an enlarged heart, and to treat kidney disease.

Azithromycin


Zithromax


Azithromycin is indicated for the treatment of patients with mild to moderate infections caused by susceptible strains of the microorganisms.It can treat various types of infections, including pink eye.

Prednisone


Deltasone


Prednisone is a synthetic, anti-inflammatory glucocorticoid that derives from cortisone.Prednisone is an delayed-release corticosteroid agent to treat a broad range of diseases and inflammations.

Ibuprofen


Advil


Ibuprofen is part of the nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) class that is used for treating pain, fever, toothache, headache, arthritis, pain, swelling, and inflammation. An OTC medication.

Hydrocodone & Acetaminophen


Norco


Hydrocodone and Acetaminophen is an opioid used to treat moderate to severe pain. Its an combination of Acetaminophen and Hydrocodone for extreme pain, it belongs to the Analgesic class. Mainly used for birth and surgery.

Gabapentin


Neurontin


Gabapentin is an anticonvulsive that adjunct to more potent anticonvulsants.It can treat pain caused by shingles and neuralgia. Able decrease seizures by decreasing abnormal excitement in the brain.
