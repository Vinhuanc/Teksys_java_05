Medications

Losartan

Generic Name: Losartan

Brand Name: Cozaar

Medication Class: Antihypertensive

Main Purpose: Blood Pressure

Prescription Quantity: 11760646

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1986

Introduction: Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) used to treat hypertension.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Nausea, Vomiting, Dizziness

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Weakness of legs
