Gabapentin

Generic Name: Gabapentin

Brand Name: Neurontin

Medication Class: Anticonvulsant

Main Purpose: Seizures

Prescription Quantity: 9818634

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1993

Introduction: Gabapentin is an anticonvulsive medication that adjunct to more potent anticonvulsants.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Dry mouth, Nausea, Vision change

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea, Swolleness of legs
