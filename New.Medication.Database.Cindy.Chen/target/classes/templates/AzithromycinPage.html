Medications

Azithromycin

Generic Name: Azithromycin

Brand Name: Zithromax

Medication Class: Antibiotic

Main Purpose: Antibiotic

Prescription Quantity: 11577286

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1980

Introduction: Azithromycin is indicated for the treatment of patients with mild to moderate infections caused by susceptible strains of the microorganisms.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Nausea, Appetite change, Dizziness

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea
