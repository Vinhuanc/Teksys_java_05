Metoprolol

Generic Name: Metoprolol

Brand Name: Lopressor

Medication Class: Antihypertensive

Main Purpose: Blood Pressure

Prescription Quantity: 15177787

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1969

Introduction: Metoprolol is an treatment for angina, heart failure, myocardial infarction, atrial fibrillation/flutter, and hypertension.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Dry mouth, Dizziness, Headache, Nausea

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Constipation, Diarrhea, Gas
