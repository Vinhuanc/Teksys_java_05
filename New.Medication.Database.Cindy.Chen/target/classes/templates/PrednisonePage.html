Prednisone

Generic Name: Prednisone

Brand Name: Deltasone

Medication Class: Anti-inflammatory

Main Purpose: Inflammation

Prescription Quantity: 10999246

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1954

Introduction: Prednisone is a synthetic, anti-inflammatory glucocorticoid that derives from cortisone.Prednisone is an FDA-approved, delayed-release corticosteroid indicated as an anti-inflammatory or immunosuppressive agent to treat a broad range of diseases.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Headache, Dizziness, Sleep changes

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea
