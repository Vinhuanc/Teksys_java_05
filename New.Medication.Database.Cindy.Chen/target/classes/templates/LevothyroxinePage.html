Medications

Levothyroxine

Generic Name: Levothyroxine

Brand Name: Synthroid

Medication Class: Thyroid

Main Purpose: Thyroid

Prescription Quantity: 19698087

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1927

Introduction: Levothyroxine is used to treat hypothyroidism. It is also used with surgery and radioactive iodine therapy to treat thyroid cancer. Levothyroxine is in a class of medications called hormones.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Weight change, Vomiting, Headache, Fever

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea, Constipation
