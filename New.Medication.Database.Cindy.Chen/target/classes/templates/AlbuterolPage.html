Medications

Albuterol

Generic Name: Albuterol

Brand Name: Ventolin

Medication Class: bronchodilators

Main Purpose: Asthma

Prescription Quantity: 19085418

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1972

Introduction: Albuterol is used to prevent and treat difficulty breathing, wheezing, shortness of breath, coughing, and chest tightness caused by lung diseases such as asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Headache, Lung pain, Heart rate change

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Urine change
