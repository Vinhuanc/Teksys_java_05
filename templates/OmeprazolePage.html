Medications

Omeprazole

Generic Name: Omeprazole

Brand Name: Prilosec

Medication Class: Proton-pump

Main Purpose: Stomach Acid

Prescription Quantity: 12869290

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1979

Introduction: Omeprazole is a proton pump inhibitor used to treat GERD associated conditions such as heartburn and gastric acid hypersecretion, and to promote healing of tissue damage and ulcers.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Headache, Nausea, Stomach pain,

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Constipation, Diarrhea
