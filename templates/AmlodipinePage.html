Medications

Amlodipine

Generic Name: Amlodipine

Brand Name: Norvasc

Medication Class: Antihypertensive

Main Purpose: Blood Pressure

Prescription Quantity: 16419181

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1982

Introduction: Amlodipine is commonly used in the treatment of high blood pressure and angina. Amlodipine has antioxidant properties and an ability to enhance the production of nitric oxide.

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Drowsiness, Nausea, Stomach pain, Headache

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea
