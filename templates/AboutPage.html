Hey there, Thanks for visiting our website!
The mission of this website is to offer free medication knowledge to users of all ages, this website contains some of the most common medications used in the world. By using this website, users will learn the following medication knowledge, generic name, brand name, medication class, main purpose, year of Approval by the FDA, short introduction of the medication, recent year prescription quantity, common side effects separated from upper body(head to waist-line) and lower body(waist-line to toe), and a sample image of the medication.

This website is user-friendly to users of all professions, from middle-school teachers to pharmacy students. This website provides common and important facts about medications either as a learning subject, or to know more about the medication your Doctor may prescribe!

Learning about medications can be challenging, but with this website, you could easily learn more about the medication by clicking the "Info" button, the knowledge you'll gain from this website can potentially be beneificial in the current or future life, the more you learn, the more knowledge you are!
