Medications

Atorvastatin

Generic Name: Atorvastatin

Brand Name: Lipitor

Medication Class: Antihypertensive

Main Purpose: cholesterol

Prescription Quantity: 24493971

Year Of Approval by the FDA: 1996

Introduction: Atorvastatin is in a class of medications called HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors (statins). It works by slowing the production of cholesterol of the heart, brain, and other parts of the body

Medication side effects of the upper body(head to waist line): Drowsiness, Fever, Appetite changes, Sleep changes

Medication side effects of the lower body(waist line to feet): Diarrhea, Constipation
